Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 1, 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all services offered by our company, including system appointment and resource management services. By using these services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What is personal data and what categories of data do we process?

The new General Data Protection Regulation establishes a more comprehensive definition of the concept of personal data. Personal data is any information, regardless of its nature and the medium in which it is presented, capable of identifying a person. These are categories of personal data processed by our company:

    Category Data Processed
    Patient Information Name, Email, Gender, Date of Birth, Phone Number, Address, Emergency Contact
    Medical Record Allergies, Medical Conditions, Appointment History

Who is responsible for processing your personal data?

Our company is responsible for processing the personal data of its users. If you have any questions or concerns about how your personal data is handled, please contact us by accessing our contact information.

In what situations do we process your personal data?

We process your personal data in the following situations:

    Storage

    We store your personal data in our database and in a secure format to provide you with the best possible service.

    Consultation

    You and the authorized professionals can consult your data to access your medical record and contact information, enabling agile and accurate service.

    Update

    The company allows you and the administrators to update data such as administrative data and medical record, keeping information always accurate.

For what purposes do we process your personal data?

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

    Purpose Reason
    Account creation The company uses your name, email, and phone number to validate your identity with the data stored in the system.
    Account presentation The company uses your email to present your account and to identify you in the system.
    Sensitive Data Change notification In the event of a change in sensitive personal data, the company uses your email and phone number to send a notification with the notice of the change in sensitive data.
    Two-step verification For better security, the system adopts a two-step verification using your email to send a confirmation in case you create an account, change account details associated with your records at the company and delete the account associated with the records.
    Scheduling surgeries The company uses your medical record to find the best possible time to schedule the surgery and to adapt all surgical resources to your needs.
    Audits The company uses your gender, data of birth and medical record to perform anonymous surveys and improve the quality of the service provided.

How do we process your personal data?

These are the circumstances that allow us to process your personal data:

    Consent

    We obtain your explicit consent to process your personal data for specific purposes. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

    Compliance with a legal obligation

    We process your personal data to comply with legal obligations, ensuring that you receive timely access to medical care as mandated by national health regulations.

    Legitimate interest

    We may process your medical record in the context of a medical malpractice lawsuit, even without your explicit consent, to protect our legitimate interests.

    Vital Interest

    In emergency situations, we access your personal data to protect your vital interests, ensuring you receive urgent medical care when needed.

    Health care

    You provide sensitive data to help us identify your surgical needs and ensure that surgeries are scheduled based on your clinical status and conditions, providing you with the best possible care.

How long do we keep your personal data?

We retain patient data for a period of 6 years, in compliance with the legal requirements set by the National Health System. If a request for data deletion is made, the data will be removed or anonymized within 30 days after the request is processed. After this period, the data will be permanently deleted or anonymized.

To whom do we transmit your personal data?

We transmit your personal data to the National Health System to ensure compliance with legal requirements and to provide you with the necessary medical care.

What are your personal data protection rights and how can you exercise them?

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

    Right Description
    Access You have the right to access your personal data and to know how it is being processed.
    Rectification You have the right to request the rectification of your personal data if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
    Erasure You have the right to request the erasure of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
    Restriction You have the right to request the restriction of the processing of your personal data when you contest the accuracy of the data or when the processing is unlawful.
    Portability You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and to transmit it to another controller.
    Objection You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data when it is based on legitimate interests.
    Automated decision-making You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you.
    Withdrawal of consent You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
    Lodging a complaint You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe that your rights have been violated.
    Exercising your rights You can exercise your rights by contacting us through the contact information provided on our website.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.
